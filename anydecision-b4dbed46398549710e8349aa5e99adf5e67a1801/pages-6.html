Here's a little about us

We all get overwhelmed with too many options. Any Decision is a tool that helps you sort through your list of options by taking it two items at a time so that you can make a decision. All you need to do is pick between the two displayed, and move on to the next. We'll sort out the rest. By the end, we'll provide your final decision and even a fancy table to show your ranked order of the remaining options. Check out our Help Section for detailed instructions.

If your group is having trouble making a decision, click on "Next Person" under the results section. Let each person go through the list of randomly paired items, and we'll compare the results at the end.

This process of grabbing a pair of items from a list and comparing them is called a "pairwise comparison." Before now, if someone wanted to make a pairwise comparison they had to do it on paper. We have decided to make this process as easy as possible by intelligently ranking items throughout the comparison process.
